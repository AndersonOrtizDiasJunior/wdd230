An expanding city

Beside its small size, the city of Mineral Town has grown 150% in the last year!


Tourism

Mineral Town is known for its beatiful mountains and hills, the most famous of the, Mother's Hill, receives visitors from all around the globe!


Strong agro industry

Mineral Town is known for its string agro culture, not only big meat corporations install themselfs here, but also a lot of independant farmers too!


Strong Jewelery industry

Because of the Lake's Mine, Mineral Town has a huge variety of jewels and ores, giving place to a huge jewelery industry.


Events

Mineral Town has a lot of fun events like horse races, cooking festivals and more!
